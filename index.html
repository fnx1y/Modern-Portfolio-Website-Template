Why Join

Staff Route

How to become a staff member

Join the community, stay active, show maturity, and apply through the staff application form. The strongest applicants are people who can stay consistent, communicate well, and contribute without needing to be chased.

What we look for

Guidelines

Community expectations

Washington State Roleplay is meant to feel more organized and more structured than a throwaway server. That only works when members and staff both carry themselves properly. The standard here should be professionalism, respect, patience, and a willingness to contribute rather than disrupt.

Staff outlook

What staff should represent

Staff should set the tone for the community rather than lower it. That means measured communication, consistent standards, and a clear understanding that leadership is about stability and quality, not just visible power.

Long-term standard

How the server should feel

The ideal outcome is a server that feels active, polished, and worth joining because people can immediately tell it has structure. Presentation matters. Clarity matters. Consistency matters. This site is part of that image, and so is the way the community is run.
